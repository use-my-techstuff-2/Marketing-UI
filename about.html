Can I Trust a Stranger With My Tech?

We try to make the Use My Techstuff community as safe as possible. We have a 5-point verification system to make sure the people on the website are who they say they are, with ratings and reviews that show previous interactions with other users. We store all transactions and interactions on our servers so you can feel confident in finalizing a transaction. We never share any information that we store for this purpose. If you still have concerns, we recommend talking to the individual before renting or lending an item from them. They could share your concerns. Remember to practice safe internet habits as you interact with people.
